About Me

I was born in the small town of St. George, Utah in the good ol USA. A town quite lacking in music. I had always been a friend to everyone, adhering to the idea that, "A friend in need is a friend indeed." Bleh, how sickenly cliche that statement is. I'm not too sure where my interest in music came from, no one in my family had anything to do with music, but somewhere down the line I decided I enjoyed it enough to at least try and make money with it. I first started with loops and mixing and matching them together to create something “unique.” Eventually getting a piano which I fell in love with and still prefer it over anything else. I eventually moved onto the drums and guitar and just overall music production and mixing.
